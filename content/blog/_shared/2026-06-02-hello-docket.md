# Hello from the docket.pub blog

This is the first post on the docket.pub blog. Going forward we'll publish a mix of
editorial coverage of specific meetings, civic explainers, and site updates here.

!!! note "What changes for you"
    Existing data, dashboards, and search behave exactly as before. The blog is
    additive — a place for narrative context that complements the structured record.

You can subscribe via [Atom feed](/blog/feed.xml) or watch a specific city's posts
via the city blog (e.g. `/al/birmingham/blog`).
